About Curl House Cleaning

An Arizona family-owned business since 2016

Our Story

Curl House Cleaning started in May 2016 as a mother and son's team with a vision of starting a business that would let us work exclusively with our family.

We believe everyone deserves a bright and clean home that is comfortable and inviting. Cleaning is hard work. Curl House Cleaning Services will provide you with a service that produces meticulous results at a modest price.

We work in teams of two cleaners allowing us to be more efficient and take less time to complete a job. We bring all of the cleaning supplies and equipment.

Together we want to create an honest, hardworking residential cleaning service that is centered around traditional family values of integrity, modesty and structure.

All of our cleaners are hand-picked and more than qualified professionals that treat your property with the same care as if it was their own.
