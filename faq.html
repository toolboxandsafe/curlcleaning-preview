❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Do you bring your own supplies?
Yes! We bring all cleaning supplies and equipment. You don't need to provide anything.
Do I need to be home during cleaning?
No, you don't need to be home. Many clients provide a key, garage code, or smart lock access. We're trustworthy and fully vetted.
How do I pay?
We accept credit/debit cards, checks, and cash. Payment is due on the day of service.
What if I'm not satisfied?
We have a 200% guarantee! Contact us within 24 hours and we'll send a different team to fix any issues. If you're still not happy, we'll refund up to the entire service price.
How many people will come?
We work in teams of two cleaners. This allows us to be more efficient and complete jobs faster.
How long does a cleaning take?
It depends on the size of your home and type of cleaning. A routine cleaning typically takes 2-4 hours. Deep cleanings take longer.
Do you clean windows?
Interior windows are available as an add-on service. We don't do exterior window cleaning.
Do you move furniture?
We don't move furniture unless the homeowner is present to help and supervise. We don't want to accidentally scratch or damage anything.
Are you insured?
Yes, we are fully insured for your protection and peace of mind.
How do I schedule?
Request a quote online or call us at 623-335-0962. We respond to 90% of quotes within 1 hour during business hours.

⚠️ What We Don't Do

  • Exterior window cleaning
  • Heavy lifting or moving large furniture
  • Biohazard or hoarding cleanup
  • Mold remediation
  • Carpet deep cleaning (steam cleaning)
  • Pest control
